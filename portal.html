How to Earn Points

We see every session you join and every shift post you respond to. We know you show up ready to work, and we wish we could dispatch everyone. We cannot, but we decide who goes and we notice the ones who keep showing up consistently.

Every time you join a session, respond to a shift post, or complete a shift, you earn points. These points build your tier and your tier determines your hourly rate. So even on the nights you do not get a shift, you are still progressing. When your turn comes, you will already be earning more because of the sessions where you showed up and waited.

Here is exactly how points work.
