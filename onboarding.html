How Glasi works

Glasi connects local bars with people like you when they get busy. Short shifts, simple work, paid the same evening. Here is exactly how it works.

The work

Glass collecting, clearing tables and keeping the bar tidy. No till work, no stocking, nothing that needs training. Wear plain black clothing, comfortable enough to move around in all evening. If you have worked in a bar before you will know the drill.

Getting selected

Not everyone who registers will be called on straight away. We select workers based on location, availability and how you come across in your application. If you have not heard from us yet, you will. We will get to you.

The session

When bars in your area are likely to need support, you will receive a Zoom link for that evening. You do not need a Zoom account, just click the link. You can join on your phone or laptop, though we would suggest laptop so your phone stays free.

Sessions run during the likely hot hours for that evening, typically a window of three to four hours. You do not need to be doing anything during that time. Just be ready to leave the moment you accept a shift.

Only join if you genuinely want to work that evening. The bars we work with expect someone who is enthusiastic, presentable and happy to be there. If you are not feeling it, that is fine. Just do not join. No penalties, no questions asked.

Confirming you are in

When you join you will be asked to read the session rules in the chat and reply with a keyword to confirm you are ready. This tells us you have read everything and you are good to go.

Staying alert

We will do sporadic check ins during the session to make sure everyone is still present and paying attention. You will not be removed for missing one straight away, but your response time is noted and it does affect the opportunities you receive. Stay alert, stay ready.

When a shift comes in

A shift will appear in the Zoom chat with the venue, the role and the shift length. To accept it, reply with the keyword shown in the post. Read the full description first. The first person to reply with the correct keyword gets the shift.

Heading to the venue

Once you have accepted you will be removed from the Zoom and you will receive a WhatsApp from us straight away confirming the venue. Reply with your live location and head straight there. We will keep the bar updated on your way.

These are big, well known venues so they will not be hard to find. We will also give you the name of who to ask for when you arrive.

Arriving

Walk straight to the entrance and tell them you are from Glasi. The manager will give you a clock in code which you send straight to us. That is your shift started.

The shift

Your shift is a fixed length agreed before you go. When your time is up, you are done. You do not need permission to leave, you have completed what was agreed.

After the shift

We review your performance with the bar after every shift. This feeds into your reliability score which affects your pay rate and the opportunities you receive over time. Turn up, do a good job, and Glasi looks after you.

Pay

You are paid by bank transfer on the same evening where possible. We run multiple pay runs per day and we will always try to catch the closest one to your shift end. If you just miss a run, payment goes out on the next one.

We are honest with you about where we are right now. As Glasi grows, so does our ability to get money to you faster. Stick with us.

You will have a login to view your payslips at any time.

We have got your back

These are popular, well known bars. That is exactly why they need support. We would not send you somewhere we would not work ourselves. If anything goes wrong on a shift, talk to us. We are on your side.
