How Glasi works

Glasi connects busy bars with people ready to step in and help at short notice. Simple work, short shifts, and fast pay.

The work

Glass collecting, clearing tables, and keeping the floor tidy.

No till work. No stocking. No training needed.

Just wear plain black clothing and something comfortable enough to move in.

Sessions (your chance to get shifts)

When we are running in your area, you will receive a Zoom link.

Sessions run during busy evening hours, usually 2 to 4 hours.

Only join if you are ready to work that night.

By joining, you are confirming that:

  • you are available
  • you are presentable
  • you can leave immediately if assigned a shift

No camera or mic needed. Everything happens in chat.

Stay alert and get more shifts

While you are in the session, stay active.

We will check in to make sure you are still ready.

The more responsive you are, the more shifts you will be considered for.

When a shift comes in

We post shifts in the Zoom chat with:

  • venue
  • shift length

You will have a short window to respond.

Reply with the keyword shown as quickly as you can.

We then choose from everyone who responded based on:

  • reliability
  • responsiveness
  • your tier

Always respond, even if you are declining.

Ignoring shifts will reduce your chances.

Earn more over time

The more reliable you are, the more you earn.

Each tier increases your hourly rate by £1.

Top workers can earn up to £16.71 per hour for the same work.

You earn points for:

  • joining sessions
  • responding to shifts
  • completing shifts
  • arriving on time

After every session, you will receive an update showing:

  • your points
  • your tier
  • how close you are to the next level

Heading to the venue

Once we assign you a shift, you will be removed from the session and sent the details on WhatsApp.

Share your live location and head straight there.

Arriving

Go to the bar or glass collection point.

Tell them you are from Glasi and ask for the named contact.

They will give you a clock-in code. Send it to us to start your shift.

The shift

Your shift is a fixed length. When it ends, you are done.

Pay

You are paid for every shift you complete.

You are not paid for time spent in sessions.

We aim to get your money to you as quickly as possible after your shift.

We have got your back

We only work with well run venues that want you there.

If anything feels off during a shift, contact us straight away.

Use the same email you registered with

This helps us match you to shifts in your area. Not a commitment.
