How shifts work

On Friday and Saturday nights over the next couple of weeks, we’ll be offering short bar shifts when venues get busy.

Bars send through requests when they need extra help, and we pass these on live.

Shifts will be at least minimum wage, last a few hours, and be no more than 20 minutes walking distance from your location — some of you may be just a few minutes away.

We guarantee venues extra help within 20 minutes, so all shifts stay within that range.

The work

The work is simple. Glass collecting, clearing tables and keeping the bar tidy.

No till work, no stocking, and nothing that requires training.

How you receive shifts

You’ll join a Zoom call during the evening, which acts as the live system.

You do not need your camera or microphone on. You can relax at home, but you must be ready to leave if you accept a shift.

When a shift comes in, it will be posted in the Zoom chat. The first person to respond “Yes - ready” gets the shift.

If you accept, you must be ready to leave immediately, share your live location via WhatsApp, and then head to the venue.

After the shift

You complete your shift and you will be paid at the end of your shift, cash or BACS, by the bar.

Important

You don’t have to accept every shift, but if you want to earn money you need to be ready and respond quickly. This is real, paid work.
