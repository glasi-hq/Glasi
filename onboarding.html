Worker Onboarding

How shifts work

On Friday and Saturday nights we'll be offering short bar shifts when venues get busy. Bars send through requests when they need extra help and we pass these on live.

Shifts will be at least minimum wage, last a few hours, and be no more than 20 minutes walking distance from your location.

We aim to have someone at the venue within 20 minutes of a request, so all shifts stay within that range.

The work

Glass collecting, clearing tables and keeping the bar tidy. No till work, no stocking, and nothing that requires training.

How you receive shifts

During the trial you'll join a Zoom call during the evening, which acts as the live dispatch system.

You do not need your camera or microphone on. You can relax at home, but you must be ready to leave immediately if you accept a shift.

You are not paid for being on the Zoom. You are only paid if you accept and complete a shift.

When a shift comes in, it will be posted in the Zoom chat. The first person to respond "Yes - ready" gets the shift.

If you accept, you must be ready to leave immediately, share your live location via WhatsApp, and head to the venue.

After the shift

You complete your shift and will be paid promptly through our payroll system. Same day pay is our goal.

Important

You don't have to accept every shift, but if you want to earn money you need to be ready and respond quickly. This is real, paid work.

What this is

This is the trial phase of Glasi. A full app is planned for later this year, which will allow you to go online, receive shift notifications, and track your earnings whenever you like.
