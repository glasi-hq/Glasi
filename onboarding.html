How Glasi works

Glasi connects busy bars with people like you when they need support. Short shifts, simple work, fast pay. Here is how it works.

The work

Glass collecting, clearing tables and keeping the bar tidy. No till work, no stocking, nothing that needs training. Wear plain black clothing and something comfortable enough to move in all evening.

Getting selected

Not everyone who registers will be invited to every session. A session is a short live window where shifts are posted for that evening. Sessions are created for specific venues and evenings, so you may not be included if you are too far from where support is needed. We select workers based on location, availability and how you come across in your application.

The session

When bars in your area are likely to need support, you will receive a Zoom link for that evening. Sessions run during the busiest hours, typically a window of three to four hours. By joining a session, you confirm that you are ready to leave immediately if you accept a shift, and that you genuinely want to work that evening. Only join if you are available, presentable and ready to go.

Confirming you are in

When you join, you will be asked to read the session rules and reply with a keyword in the chat to confirm you are ready. You do not need your camera or mic on. We only use the chat in Zoom. This confirms that you have read everything and are good to go.

Staying alert

We will check in during the session to make sure everyone is still present and paying attention. Your response time is noted and affects the opportunities you receive.

When a shift comes in

When a shift becomes available, it will be posted in the chat with the venue and shift length. These requests come in when bars need someone quickly, so response time matters. To accept a shift, reply with the keyword shown in the post. The first person to reply with the correct keyword gets the shift. Always accept or decline a shift. Do not ignore it.

We track response time, reliability and completed shifts. Workers who are consistently responsive and reliable are promoted through the tiers quickly. Each tier increases your hourly rate by £1. In a short time, you could be earning up to £16.71 per hour for the same work.

Heading to the venue

Once you have accepted a shift, you will be removed from the Zoom and sent the venue details on WhatsApp. Reply with your live location and head straight there. We will keep the venue updated on your arrival.

Arriving

When you arrive, head to the glass collection point or the pass at the bar. Let them know you are from Glasi and ask for the person named in your WhatsApp. You will be given a clock-in code, which you send to us to start your shift.

The shift

Your shift is a fixed length agreed before you go. When your time is up, you are done.

After the shift

After each shift, we review your performance with the venue. This feeds into your reliability score, which determines the opportunities you receive and how quickly you move up through the tiers and earn more.

Pay

You are paid for every shift you complete. You are not paid for time spent in sessions. You choose when to join, and only join if you want to work that evening. We aim to get money to you as quickly as possible after your shift.

We have got your back

We only work with well run, high volume venues that want you there. If anything feels off during a shift, contact us straight away. We are on your side.
