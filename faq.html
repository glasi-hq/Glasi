About Glasi

Glasi is an on-demand floor support platform connecting busy bars with reliable local people. When a bar gets busy and needs extra hands, they contact Glasi and we send someone down within minutes to collect glasses, clear tables, and keep the floor moving.
We are launching in Southampton first, with Portsmouth and Winchester to follow. If you are based in or near Southampton, now is the perfect time to register.
Yes. Glasi Ltd is a registered UK company (Company No. 17147716). All workers are employed on a PAYE basis through a professional payroll partner. You receive payslips, your tax and National Insurance are handled correctly, and your employment is fully above board.

The Work

Glass collecting, clearing tables, and keeping the floor tidy. No till work, no stocking, nothing that needs training. Just wear plain black clothing and be ready to work hard for a few hours.
Bar or hospitality experience is helpful but not essential. What matters most is reliability, a good attitude, and being comfortable in a fast-paced environment.
Shifts are typically 2 to 4 hours. The length is agreed before you head to the venue. When your time is up, you are done.
Sessions run during the busiest hours at bars, typically Friday and Saturday evenings. You will receive a Zoom link in advance when a session is running in your area.

Pay

Starting pay is £12.71 per hour. As you build your reputation and move up through the tiers, your rate increases by £1 per hour at each level. The top tier pays £16.71 per hour for the same work.
We aim to get money to you as quickly as possible after your shift. You are paid via PAYE through our payroll partner, so your tax and National Insurance are handled automatically.
No. You are only paid for shifts you complete. However, joining sessions and responding to shift posts earns you points toward your next tier, which means a higher hourly rate when you do get a shift.

Tiers and Progression

Glasi has five tiers. Everyone starts at Tier 1 (£12.71/hr). As you build your track record you earn points and progress through the tiers. Tier 5 pays £16.71 per hour for the same work. The better you perform, the faster you move up.
You earn points for joining sessions, responding to shift posts, completing shifts, and arriving on time. After every session you will receive a personal update showing your points, your tier, and how close you are to the next level.
We can't dispatch everyone, but we do notice. You still earn points just for showing up and responding. Those points build toward your next tier, so when you do get a shift you will already be earning more.

How It Works

When a session is running in your area you will receive a Zoom link. Join if you are ready to work that night. When a shift becomes available it is posted in the Zoom chat. You have a short window to respond with the keyword shown. We then select from everyone who responded, taking into account reliability, responsiveness, and tier. If you are assigned the shift, you will be sent the venue details on WhatsApp.
Head to the bar or glass collection point. Tell them you are from Glasi and ask for the named contact in your WhatsApp. They will give you a clock-in code, which you send to us to start your shift.
Contact us straight away. We only work with well run venues that want you there, and we are on your side. If anything feels off, let us know immediately.
